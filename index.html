Primus the Oncologist

About

Primus serves as the Programme Director for Oncology Postgraduate training at the University of Nairobi. He holds dual roles as a lecturer and a consultant clinical oncologist at Kenyatta National Hospital. Before assuming this position, he underwent a placement at Christie Oncology NHS Trust (UK) to gain insights into Postgraduate medical education. Currently, Primus is pursuing fellowship training in advanced Radiotherapy, specializing in Cyberknife, stereotactic radiotherapy, and interstitial brachytherapy in India. With a strong inclination toward research, Primus serves as the principal site investigator for the Unitid oncology clinical trial site at the University of Nairobi. In his pursuit of excellence, he actively collaborates with various training centers in the developed world, focusing on areas such as training, research, and skills development.
